Recipe

Choclate cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Choclate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"


Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black choclate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

 Melt the choclate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smouth dough

4

 Bake in the oven
